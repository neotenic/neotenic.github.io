About

Protobowl is a real time multiplayer quizbowl application launched in late August 2012.

This was concieved some time around May 2012, but real development didn't start until July. The name is a portmanteau of "prototype" and "quizbowl".

I implore you to contact info@protobowl.com with any and all questions, comments or concerns, preferably those which relate to Protobowl.
